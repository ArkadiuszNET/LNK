LNK Privacy policy

Last modified 30 July 2023

Lock'n Knowledge respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose personal data about you when you use our mobile application ("App").



1. Personal Data We Collect


We may collect certain personal data about you when you use our App, including:

  • Device information: We may collect details about the type of device you are using to access our App, such as the device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Ad identifiers: To display personalized ads based on your interests, our App may use Google Ads and collect your advertising identifier. On iOS devices, this is known as "IDFA" (Identifier for Advertising), and on Android devices, it is referred to as "Android Advertising ID.
  • User-provided information: If you choose to provide us with information, such as when you submit a support request, participate in a survey, or provide feedback, we will collect and process that information.
  • Photos: Our App may offer features or functions that require access to your device's photos. However, it's important to note that we do not collect or transmit any of your photos. All such photo data remains securely stored on your device and is not shared with us.
  • We respect your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your data. Rest assured that we do not collect any personal data beyond what is necessary for displaying Google Ads within the App. Your trust is essential to us, and we prioritize the protection of your information.



2. How We Use Your Personal Data


We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our App: We use your personal data to operate, maintain, and improve our App, including to personalize your experience.
  • To show personalized ads: With your consent, we use your advertising identifier to show you personalized ads based on your interests.
  • To communicate with you: We may use your personal data to respond to your inquiries or requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations: We may use your personal data to comply with applicable laws, regulations, and court orders.



3. How We Share Your Personal Data


We may share your personal data with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent: We may share your personal data with third parties if you have given us your consent to do so.
  • Service providers: We may share your personal data with service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, analytics, and customer support.
  • Legal requirements: We may share your personal data if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or court orders.


4. Use of Google Ads


The App uses Google Ads to display advertising. Google Ads may collect certain information about you, such as your device type, IP address, and browsing activity, in order to display more relevant ads. For more information about how Google Ads collects and uses data, please see Google's privacy policy.



5. Your Rights


If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have certain rights with respect to your personal data, including:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal data we have about you. If you have provided us with any personal data during your interaction with our App, we will provide you with details of the information we have collected upon request.
  • Rectification: If any of the personal data we hold about you is inaccurate or incomplete, you can ask us to correct or update it to ensure its accuracy.
  • Erasure: If you have previously provided personal data, but no longer wish to have that information stored or processed by us, you can request the erasure of your data. It's important to note that if you have not provided any personal data and have not interacted with us, there is no data associated with you that requires erasure.
  • Restriction: You can ask us to restrict the processing of your personal data if you believe that its processing is unlawful or if you contest its accuracy. If you haven't provided us with any personal data and have not interacted with us, there is no data that requires restriction.
  • Data Portability: If you have provided personal data, you can request that we provide you with a copy of your information in a machine-readable format, allowing you to transfer it to another service or platform.
  • Please note that if you have never provided any personal data and have not interacted with us, there is no data associated with you, and therefore, no action is required on your part. Should you wish to remove any personal data provided in the past, you may do so by uninstalling the App from your device.


6. Data Retention


We will retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, unless a longer retention period is required by law.



7. Children's Privacy


The App is child-friendly and does not contain any harmful content. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have collected personal data of a child under the age of 13, we will take steps to delete such information as soon as possible.



8. Changes to This Privacy Policy


We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time by posting a new version on our website or within our App. We will notify you of any material changes to this Privacy Policy by posting a notice within our App or by sending you an email. Your continued use of our App after any such changes will constitute your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.



9. Contact Us


If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at ignition.net@gmail.com
